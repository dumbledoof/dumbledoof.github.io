ASRA SIDDIQUI

I love giving audiences a taste of my mind, of all the craziness that goes on in there, whether it be through works of code, sound, animation, illustration, photography or anything else. The set of mediums and techniques I utilize is always growing, as I am constantly exploring new ways to convey my art. Some of my platforms include Processing, Audacity, and the Adobe Suite. Through my art pieces, I wish to tell stories and make statements that resonate with people who view my projects, as well as with myself. Many of my projects are inspired by whatever had been taking up my mind at that time, such as a stressful job at a pizza shop, a recent breakup, or even just my constant contemplations of the universe.

My art is fundamentally a display of my personality. My inspirations are compositions of literally everything that I experience. I love ruminating on all the inner workings of my mind and body, events that I go through in life, and works of other artists that I come across. I just want to share with everyone the stories of my life, hoping they might find my work relatable or inspirational in any way.
